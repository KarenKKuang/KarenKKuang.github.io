Apple Harvest Festival

Admist COVID-19, the 2020 Apple Festive will be Distant and De-densified. Be Safe & Socially Responsible while enjoying the festival!

Be Safe & Socially Distant & Responsible

Downtown encourages all customers and farmers to take responsibility for their own health and the health of others by adopting the following actions to prevent the spread of COVID-19:

  1. New York state requires everyone to wear a face covering when in public and unable to maintain physical distancing measures.
  2. Cover your cough or sneeze with a tissue, then throw the tissue in the trash.
  3. Lines with 6' or more of social distance between customers will be in place at the farm stands.
  4. Hand sanitizer is stationed around the Ithaca Commons, and patrons are encouraged to bring their own for personal use as well.
