Apple Harvest Open Air Farmers Market

Downtown Ithaca will welcome a couple farmers to sell their harvest bounty throughout the week in a socially distant, de-densified version of a farmers market.

During the day, the farms will have pop-up farm stands each afternoon on the commons to sell pre-packed Apples, Cider and other fall produce for contactless grab & go.

Participating Vendors:

  • Robbies Produce Farm Market
  • AJ Teeter Farm
  • Littletree Orchards
  • Baked Up & Bubbled Over
  • Bakers Acres
  • Creamcycle

Apple & Cider Trail

Celebrate the apple season this year by enjoy a socially distant, contactless, taste-tastic journey of sipping, noshing, & shopping.

Apple & Cider Trail is now available! Simply save the map, print & make your plans and let the fruitful week get started. Snap a photo of your experiences throughout the week and share them with us via Instagram or Facebook. Tag us @downtownithaca and hashtag it #FeelingAppleFestive to enter to win. Enjoy ciders by the glass with your meal, juice for purchase, fun apple-inspired novelties, and even sweet and savory dishes showcasing the mighty fruit!
