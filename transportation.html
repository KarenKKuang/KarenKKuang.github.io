Parking Information

Residents and vistors can park on the street or park in the Green and Seneca Street garages and walk to anywhere in Downtown Ithaca.

On-street Parking

On-street parking is $1.50/hour during the week until 6pm.

Green Street Garage

Location: 120 East Green Street

Distance from the Farmer's Market: 26 minutes walk

Parking Rate: $1.00/ hour

For more information, visit Green Street Garage's Page

E. Seneca Street Garage

Location: 202 E. Seneca Street

Distance from the Farmer's Market: 22 minutes walk

Parking Rate: $1.00/ hour

For more information, visit Seneca Street Garage's Page
